About HBM Building Maintenance

HBM Building Maintenance is a Toronto-based company specializing in residential and commercial maintenance services since 2015. We provide comprehensive solutions for high-rise window cleaning, glass repair, concrete testing, rope access training, and much more across Canada and the U.S. Our commitment to precision, quality, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for building maintenance needs of all sizes.

We believe in hiring for attitude and training for skill, maintaining an 8:1 hiring ratio to ensure only the most qualified professionals join our team. Our uniformed, trained, and polite staff follow our core values: Do What You Said, Finish What You Began, Be on Time, Say Please and Thank You, and Work Fast with Quality. From ground-level maintenance to sky-high installations, we deliver reliable services that keep buildings safe, functional, and aesthetically pleasing.
